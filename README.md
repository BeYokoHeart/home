**BeYokoHeart** was created with the vision of building a place where everyone can freely access automated tools that support personal growth, self-reflection, and simplified interpretations of systems such as the **I Ching**, cards, and other traditions used around the world.

The project will be gradually expanded with additional self-discovery tools and communication features, creating a single, continuously growing space that is accessible to everyone.

Free Access
Our guiding principle is a simple goal:
As long as a given tool operates fully automatically and does not require human involvement, it will remain available free of charge to every user.

We believe that knowledge and the opportunity to explore various automated systems should not be limited by paid access.

Project Development
We are developing the project at our own pace, and its progress depends on the time, resources, and opportunities available to us.

Your support allows us to create new tools, improve existing features, and bring new ideas to life.

Supporters
